## Standard Content Page Template

### 1. Page Header
- Title
- Short neutral definition

### 2. Summary Panel
- What it is
- Who it affects
- Key clinical facts

### 3. Evidence Level
- High / Moderate / Limited
- Explanation of classification

### 4. Detailed Sections
- Background
- Causes / Mechanisms
- Symptoms / Presentation
- Diagnosis
- Treatment Options (descriptive, non-recommendational)
- Prognosis

### 5. Citations
- Inline numbered references

### 6. References
- Peer-reviewed studies
- Guidelines

### 7. Metadata
- Last reviewed date
- Editorial responsibility note

## Landing Page Specification
- Must not overwhelm
- Focus on trust and clarity
- No imagery suggesting cosmetic outcomes

## Accessibility & SEO
- Semantic HTML
- Clear heading hierarchy
- Descriptive meta titles (non-promotional)
