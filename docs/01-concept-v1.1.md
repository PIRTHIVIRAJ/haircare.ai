## Purpose
HairCare.ai is a neutral, evidence-based hair health knowledge platform. Its sole objective is to educate users using verifiable medical and scientific sources. The platform explicitly avoids product sales, affiliate links, brand promotion, or cosmetic marketing language.

## Core Principles
- Evidence-first: All claims must be traceable to peer-reviewed research, clinical guidelines, or authoritative medical consensus.
- Neutrality: No endorsements, rankings, or recommendations of commercial products or services.
- Transparency: Clear disclaimers, evidence grading, and citation visibility.
- Accessibility: Content written for lay understanding without sacrificing scientific accuracy.

## Target Users
- General public researching hair and scalp conditions
- Individuals experiencing hair loss or scalp disorders
- Clinicians and students seeking structured summaries
- Journalists and researchers looking for neutral references

## Content Scope
- Hair biology and growth cycles
- Hair loss types (androgenetic alopecia, telogen effluvium, alopecia areata, scarring alopecias)
- Scalp conditions (dandruff, psoriasis, folliculitis)
- Treatments and interventions (medical, procedural, lifestyle)
- Diagnostics and evaluation methods

## Content Constraints
- No ecommerce or lead generation
- No calls to action related to products or clinics
- No anecdotal or influencer-style content

## Trust & Compliance
- Medical disclaimer displayed globally
- Clear distinction between evidence levels
- Periodic review and update timestamps
